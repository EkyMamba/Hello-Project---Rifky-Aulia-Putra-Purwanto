hr

The hr HTML element represents a thematic break between paragraph-level elements: for example, a change of scene in a story, or a shift of topic within a section.

Example:

Despacito

Despacito

Despacito

Despacito

Despacito
Despacito

br

The br HTML element produces a line break in text (carriage-return). It is useful for writing a poem or an address, where the division of line is significant.
